# 🤝 For Perspective Students

I thrive in collaborative and interdisciplinary research environments, working with experts across fields to broaden the impact of my work. My collaborations include leaders in marine robotics, such as [Prof. Shahriar Negahdaripour](https://people.miami.edu/profile/f99d45312215a158b90e333b23d8bfd2) (Miami University), [Prof. Ioannis Rekleitis](https://sc.edu/study/colleges_schools/engineering_and_computing/faculty-staff/ioannisrekleitis.php) (University of South Carolina), [Prof. Herbert Tanner](https://research.me.udel.edu/~btanner/) (University of Delaware), and [Prof. Jane Shine](https://mae.ufl.edu/people/faculty/primary/profiles/jane-jaejeong-shin/) (University of Florida). 

I have had a fantastic opportunity to collaborate with experts from various fields worldwide, such as biologist [Dr. Isla Duporge](https://scholar.google.co.uk/citations?user=oJ_KilcAAAAJ&hl=en) (Princeton), AI researcher [Prof. Olga Isupova](https://olgaisupova.github.io/) (University of Bath), computer scientist [Prof. Michail G. Lagoudakis](https://www.intelligence.tuc.gr/~lagoudakis/) (Technical University of Crete), computer scientist [Prof. Markus Vincze](https://www.acin.tuwien.ac.at/en/staff/vm/) (Technical University Wien (TUW)), and biological and agricultural researcher [Prof. Dongyi Wang](https://sfelab.uark.edu/about/) (University of Arkansas).

I also have had the honor to work with experts in Maryland, such as ecophysiologist [Prof. Matthew Gray](https://www.umces.edu/matthew-gray) (University of Maryland Center for Environment Science (UMCES)), aquaculture specialist [Mr. Don Webster](https://www.linkedin.com/in/don-webster-7904b88/) (UMCES), mechanical engineer and researcher [Prof. Nikhil Chopra](https://terpconnect.umd.edu/~nchopra/Home.html) (University of Maryland), roboticist [Prof. Pratap Tokekar](https://tokekar.com/) (University of Maryland), and ecologist [Dr. Jason E. Spires](https://coastalscience.noaa.gov/staff/jason-spires/) (NOAA Cooperative Oxford Laboratory).

Taking initiative is key to my work; for example, at the 2024 Autonomous Systems Bootcamp, I led a team from eight institutions and companies, culminating in our joint publication on the **Odyssee** project as the lead and corresponding author.
