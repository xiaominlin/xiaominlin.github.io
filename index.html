I am a researcher in robotics and AI, focusing on underwater robotics, edge AI, and multimodal perception. My research group (ERA Lab) aims to build intelligent systems for complex real-world environments with long-term autonomy.

I am also developing open-source tools for embodied AI and edge computing. I collaborate with academic, industry, and government partners, and mentor a growing group of graduate and undergraduate students.

Current Research Areas

  • Underwater robotics and multimodal sensing.
  • Edge AI and efficient deep learning.
  • Long-term autonomous environmental monitoring.
  • Large Language Models for robotics reasoning.

Recognition

My work has been recognized with Best Paper and Best Poster Awards (IROS, Maryland Robotics Symposium), and I serve as Associate Editor and Workshop Organizer for ICRA/IROS/AAAI.
